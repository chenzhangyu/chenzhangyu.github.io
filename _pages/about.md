Zhangyu Chen is a researcher and engineer at **Huawei Cloud**, where he focuses on building efficient cloud infrastructure for large language models (LLMs). He received both his Ph.D (advised by [Prof. Yu Hua](https://csyhua.github.io/csyhua/index.html)) and B.E degrees from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST).

His research interests include **AI infrastructure**, **LLM inference**, **storage systems**, and **debugging**.
