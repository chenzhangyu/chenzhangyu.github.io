Zhangyu Chen (陈章玉)  [CV]

CS Ph.D student
Huazhong University of Science and Technology


Biography:Zhangyu Chen is a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He obtained the B.E degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2017.

His research interests include computer system & architecture, non-volatile memories, and debugging, which focus on building correct and high-performance storage systems using non-volatile memory technologies.
