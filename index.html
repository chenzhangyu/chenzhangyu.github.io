Zhangyu Chen (陈章玉)

Huawei Cloud


Biography: Zhangyu Chen is a researcher and engineer at Huawei Cloud, where he focuses on building efficient cloud infrastructure for large language models (LLMs). He received both his Ph.D (advised by Prof. Yu Hua) and B.E degrees from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST).

His research interests include AI infrastructure, LLM inference, storage systems, and debugging.
